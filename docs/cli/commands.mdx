---
title: CLI Commands
description: "Manage your relay, channels, and policies from the terminal."
---

The Grat CLI is the easiest way to interact with your relay server and manage your infrastructure.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Setup">
    - `grat init`: Walk through the initial configuration.
    - `grat config set`: Update specific configuration values.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fee Channels">
    - `grat channels list`: Show the health and balance of all channels.
    - `grat channels fund`: Send XLM from a source account to all channels.
    - `grat channels health`: Verify network status for every channel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Policies">
    - `grat policy list`: View active sponsorship rules.
    - `grat policy test <XDR>`: Test an XDR against your rules without submitting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitoring">
    - `grat status`: General relay and network health.
    - `grat logs --follow`: Stream real-time relay logs to your terminal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

> [!TIP]
> Run `grat --help` to see all available commands and flags.
