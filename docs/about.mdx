## Why Grat Exists

Launchtube was deprecated and left a gap in the Stellar ecosystem. Developers building consumer apps on Stellar suddenly had no maintained, open-source way to sponsor transaction fees for their users. Grat exists to fill that gap permanently — as open-source infrastructure the ecosystem can depend on.

## Open Source Commitment

Grat is MIT-licensed. The relay server, all SDKs, the CLI, Docker configs, and documentation are public on GitHub. We believe gas abstraction is infrastructure that should be open and self-hostable, not locked behind a proprietary service. Contributions are welcome.

## Stellar Ecosystem

Grat is built specifically for Stellar and Soroban. It uses Stellar's native fee-bump transaction mechanism (CAP-0015) rather than wrapping a generic relayer around a Stellar-incompatible design. It's funded through the Stellar Community Fund and designed to serve the Stellar developer ecosystem long-term.
