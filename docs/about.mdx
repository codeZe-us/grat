### Why Grat Exists

The deprecation of Launchtube by the Stellar Development Foundation created a significant gap in the ecosystem. Independent developers and small teams were left without a standard, open-source solution for sponsoring transaction fees. Grat was built to fill this gap, providing a robust and permissive infrastructure that anyone can use to build zero-gas experiences.

### Our Mission

We believe that blockchain technology will only reach mass adoption when the "complexity" of the network becomes invisible to the end user. Grat is our contribution to making Stellar the most user-friendly network for real-world digital payments.
