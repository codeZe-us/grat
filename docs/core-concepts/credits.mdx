title: Credits

---

Grat uses a simple credit-based system to track and manage your sponsorship costs.

### How it works
1. **Deposit**: You deposit XLM into your Grat account via the dashboard or CLI.
2. **Convert**: XLM is tracked as **Credits** measured in **Stroops** (1 XLM = 10,000,000 Credits).
3. **Deduct**: When you sponsor a transaction, the exact fee paid to the Stellar network is deducted from your credit balance in real-time.

### Why Credits?
- **Predictability**: Set low-balance alerts so you never run out of sponsorship capacity.
- **Transparency**: Every credit deduction is linked to a specific transaction hash and requestId.
- **Fairness**: You only pay for the exact fees the network charges; Grat does not add "gas markups."

> [!NOTE]
> Detailed credit tracking and dashboard features are currently part of the **Phase 2** roadmap and will be enabled for Mainnet integrations.
