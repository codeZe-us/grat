## Self-Hosting is Always Free

Grat is Apache 2.0 licensed and open-source. You can self-host the relay server on your own infrastructure at no cost, forever. Docker, Kubernetes, Railway, Render, Fly.io - deploy wherever you want. The managed cloud is for teams who would rather not manage infrastructure. Check out our [Self-Hosting Guide](/guides/self-hosting) to get started.

## How Credits Work

All plans include a transaction allowance. Beyond that, you deposit XLM credits and Grat deducts the actual network fee per sponsored transaction. Average Stellar fees are 100 stroops (0.00001 XLM), so credits go far. You can deposit via Stellar payment or credit card. Balance and spending are fully visible in the dashboard and via API.
